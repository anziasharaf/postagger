 POS Tagger
 
    Part-of-speech tagging (POS tagging ), also called grammatical tagging or word-category disambiguation, is the process of marking up a word in a text(corpus) as corresponding to a particular part of speech, based on both its definition and its context â€” i.e., its relationship with adjacent and related words in a phrase, sentence, or paragraph. A TnT tagger is customized and trained for malayalam.TnT is a statistical POS tagger that can be trainable on different languages.
